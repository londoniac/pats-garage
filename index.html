What We Do

Our Services

01

MOT Testing

Full DVSA-approved MOT testing for Class 4 vehicles. We'll give your car a thorough check and advise on any issues honestly — no upselling, just straightforward results. Saturday morning slots available.

02

Full Servicing

Interim and full services to manufacturer specifications. We use quality parts and keep your service history up to date so your warranty stays intact. Competitive rates with no dealer markup.

03

Mechanical Repairs

From brake pads and clutches to suspension and exhaust work — we handle all mechanical repairs on most makes and models. We always explain the problem before we start any work, and we'll never replace parts you don't need.

04

Engine Diagnostics

Dashboard warning light on? Our diagnostic equipment reads fault codes from all major systems — engine, ABS, airbag, transmission and more. We'll pinpoint the issue quickly so you're not paying for guesswork.

05

Tyres & Brakes

Tyre fitting, balancing and alignment plus full brake inspections and replacement. We source competitively-priced tyres from trusted suppliers and can usually fit same-day. Your safety comes first.

06

Pre-Purchase Inspections

Thinking of buying a used car? Bring it to us first. We'll give it a thorough independent check and tell you exactly what condition it's in — could save you thousands on a bad purchase.

About Us

Your Local
Trusted Mechanic

Pat's Garage has been looking after vehicles in East Grinstead since 2008. We're a genuinely independent garage — no franchise fees, no corporate targets — which means we can focus entirely on giving you honest advice and quality work at a fair price.

Based on Charlwoods Road, we're easy to find and have built our reputation on straightforward service. We treat every car as if it were our own and we'll always talk you through what needs doing before we pick up a spanner.

Whether you need a routine MOT, a full service, or help tracking down an intermittent fault, we're here to help. We work on most makes and models and we're always happy to give free, no-obligation advice over the phone.
